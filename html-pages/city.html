Honda City

Exterior
The Honda City 2021 exterior features a unique and distinctive design language. The front end houses sleek narrow trapezium style side swept headlights, an angular grille and dual air intakes. The rear end houses the trapezium styled side swept headlights, a chrome trim piece running the length of the trunk lid and a large rear bumper. The facelifted versions of the feature a chrome angular grille at the front and a new set of taillights with rectangular inserts and a new larger rear bumper. The overall Honda City 2021 exterior stand out amongst its rivals, especially in the local market. Interior
New Model Honda City 2021 interior compromises of black and silver plastic trim pieces before the facelift now it comes in beige color. The front and rear seats are covered in fabric. Standard features on the standard variant include air conditioning, power windows, power steering, power lock doors, 2-speaker stereo audio system, power windows, trip computer, cup holders and a tachometer. Features on the higher end Aspire variant includes alloy wheels, Satellite navigation as standard and front fog lights. Options include leather upholstery, satellite navigation and a 4-speaker stereo audio system. The overall Honda City 2021 interior offers a good level of comfort and noise insulation.

The Honda City 2021 can achieve an estimated 18 Km/L on highways and 12 to 16 Km/L of average within the city. Honda City is quite a famous car in 1300cc cars category.
