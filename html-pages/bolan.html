Suzuki Bolan


Exterior

The exterior of the Suzuki Bolan 2021 features a fairly boxy design. The flat front fascia features square headlights with a rectangular styled black grille and rectangular fog lights integrated into the black bumper. The rear end of the Bolan features rectangular stacked taillights with a black bumper. All variants of the Suzuki Bolan feature identical styling in every aspect. The overall exterior of the Bolan is very tall, square and fairly van like, familiar design for a mini MPV designed in the early nineties

Interior

The Suzuki Bolan Carry Daba interior features the use of grey tone plastic trim pieces. The car lacks basic features, and everything is manually operated. The seats are covered in vinyl and can to accommodate seven people. Features available in the Bolan include air conditioning and fabric seat upholstery. The overall interior features a good amount of interior space but below par interior ride quality, comfort and noise insulation.

Engine

  • The Bolan is offered in a choice of 1 engine 0.8 liter 6 Valve four-stroke 3-cyclinder engine
Mileage

The Suzuki Bolan 2021 gives an average 12 KM/L in city driving and 14 KM/L on the highway making for an estimated range of 432KM in the city and 504KM on the highway.
Competitor

The main competitors of the Suzuki Bolan 2021 is the FAW XPV.
